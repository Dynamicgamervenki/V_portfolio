Motion Builder Essentials
Characterization & Retargeting

Characterization
Characterization is the process of defining a character’s skeleton in Autodesk MotionBuilder by mapping each bone/joint (such as hips, spine, arms, and legs) to MotionBuilder’s standard character template. This mapping allows MotionBuilder to recognize the skeleton as a valid “Character,” enabling advanced animation tools such as Control Rigs, IK/FK manipulation, and animation retargeting.

Retargeting
Retargeting is the process of transferring animation data from a source character to a target character in MotionBuilder. Once both characters are properly characterized, MotionBuilder can reinterpret the source animation (motion capture or keyframed animation) and apply it to the target skeleton.
