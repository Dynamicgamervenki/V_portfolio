Override


A hacking-driven top-down stealth prototype developed in Unity in under 10 days. Inspired by Watch Dogs, this project focuses on environmental interaction and system manipulation rather than direct combat, encouraging players to think tactically and experiment with different approaches.

Key Features:
• Multiple hacking actions: Toggle, Hack, Hijack, Stun, Switch, Destroy
• Interactive environments with doors, terminals, and laser barriers
• Enemy drones and security systems that can be disabled or controlled
• Puzzle-oriented level design promoting creative problem solving
• Rapid prototype built to explore core gameplay mechanics and player abilities
• Implemented using C# scripts in Unity
